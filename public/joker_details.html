Joker - Character Page

Movie

Joker is a character from the movie "Joker" directed by Todd Phillips.

Story

"Joker" tells the story of Arthur Fleck, a failed stand-up comedian who turns to a life of crime and chaos in Gotham City.

Character

Joker, also known as Arthur Fleck, is a compelling character due to his complex psychology and portrayal by Joaquin Phoenix. He embodies chaos and unpredictability.

  • He challenges societal norms and conventions.
  • His struggles with mental health shed light on important social issues.
  • His transformation from a marginalized individual to a symbol of anarchy is captivating.
